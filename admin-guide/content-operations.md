title: Content Operations

---

Editorial work in ADMIN9 lives here when the change should be made through `/admin` rather than through a deployment.

## Content areas in the admin panel

The admin resources include:

- Blog Posts
- Blog Post Categories
- Announcements
- FAQs
- Roadmap Items

Treat these resources as part of launch and ongoing operations, not as optional extras.

## Blog

The blog system includes:

- `Blog Posts`
- `Blog Post Categories`

Use this area to:

- publish launch or product education content
- organize posts by category
- support SEO and discoverability

Before launch:

- review post slugs
- review category naming
- remove drafts or placeholder copy
- verify any seeded content is intentional

## Announcements

Announcements are the right place for in-product or account-area communication.

Use them for:

- launch notices
- maintenance messaging
- feature highlights
- billing or policy reminders

Before release:

- confirm active announcements are current
- remove internal-only messaging
- verify timing and placement strategy

## FAQs

FAQs are especially important for first release because they reduce support load.

Use this resource to cover:

- pricing questions
- billing behavior
- account management
- refunds or cancellation expectations
- onboarding basics

Before launch:

- remove placeholder entries
- make answers product-accurate
- align wording with legal and billing policy

## Roadmap

The roadmap system supports public-facing feature suggestion and voting workflows.

Use it intentionally. On first release, decide whether roadmap visibility is:

- part of your product strategy
- limited to curated items
- deferred until after launch

Before launch:

- verify all visible roadmap items are public-safe
- remove internal planning notes
- confirm statuses are meaningful to users

## Ownership

These resources should usually have a clear owner:

- product marketing for blog and announcements
- product or support for FAQs
- product management for roadmap visibility

If ownership is unclear, content systems tend to decay quickly after launch.
