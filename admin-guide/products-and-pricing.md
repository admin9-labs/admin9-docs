Come here to review the catalog and pricing surfaces that shape what customers can purchase.

## Core resources

The admin panel includes resources for:

- Products
- Plans
- One-Time Products
- Discounts
- Subscriptions
- Orders
- Transactions
- Balances
- Balance Transactions

## Operating model

### Products

Use products to define the main commercial offering exposed on the site.

Typical responsibilities:

- create a product shell
- control product visibility and presentation
- connect products to plans where relevant

### Plans

Plans represent recurring subscription offers.

Typical responsibilities:

- define tier structure
- manage pricing relations
- manage provider-specific plan data when required

### One-time products

Use one-time products for non-recurring purchases.

Typical responsibilities:

- maintain standalone sellable items
- manage their pricing
- manage provider-specific product identifiers if required

### Discounts

Discounts are used for promotional and retention scenarios.

The discount resource also includes discount code management through a dedicated relation manager.

### Subscriptions and orders

These resources are primarily for review, support, and operational intervention rather than catalog setup.

Use them to:

- inspect lifecycle state
- review customer activity
- troubleshoot billing issues
- inspect usage or related transactional data

## Pre-launch checklist

- confirm every public plan has the correct pricing rows
- confirm provider-specific plan and product mappings exist
- confirm discounts are scoped and dated correctly
- verify test purchases for both subscriptions and one-time products
- verify invoices and transaction records appear as expected
