# Content and Site Settings

ADMIN9 includes a substantial amount of editable site and product content in the admin panel.

## Content resources

Current content-oriented resources include:

- Blog Posts
- Blog Post Categories
- Announcements
- FAQs
- Roadmap Items
- Referrals

Use these to manage public-facing content and customer communication.

## Site settings pages

The panel also includes standalone settings pages for:

- General Settings
- Invoice Settings
- Legal Pages Settings
- Open Graph Image Settings
- Referral Settings

## What belongs here

### General Settings

Use this area for broad site-level behavior and presentation settings. The codebase also indicates it includes settings related to reCAPTCHA.

### Legal Pages Settings

Use this area to maintain the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy content rendered on public routes.

### Invoice Settings

Use this area to control invoice-related behavior and presentation.

### Open Graph Image Settings

Use this area to maintain share-image defaults or generation-related behavior for SEO and social sharing.

### Referral Settings

Use this area to tune referral behavior and incentives.

## Editorial release checklist

- verify blog content and categories
- verify FAQ accuracy
- verify roadmap content is intentional and public-safe
- verify announcements are current
- verify terms and privacy content are production-ready
- verify Open Graph assets reflect the release brand
- verify referral settings match the commercial launch plan
